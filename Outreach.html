Outreach and Leadership

Both in undergrad and grad school, I served in various leadership roles and outreach efforts, with a specific focus on increasing gender diversity in physics. I also have spent time trying to prepare fellow graduate students and postdocs for careers after graduation, both in academia and outisde of it. My volunteer efforts include meeting with congressional staff to discuss funding of particle physics, helping out at Conference for Undergraduate Women in Physics, and leading groups of high school and middle school students through physics activities at Yale Physics Olympics and Girl's Science Investigations
